Loiter

Working in a group setting, my team and I worked on a concept of developing a companion piece to SeatGeek. Using the API from SeatGeek and HERE, we created a event browser that takes searches the given parameters and pulls events similar to, nearby, or based on the query. Based off of the results, you can click on any of the options and a map will populate various bars and eateries around the event.
