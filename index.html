About Me

I was formerly working as a QA Lead for a company that builds online ordering websites for various restaurants. There, my responsibilities included:

  • Building up the back end for the online menus through the company's proprietary program
  • Maintaining websites to ensure that they were displaying content correctly on the front end
  • Communicating with new clients regarding the look and feel for their website
  • Working with the programming team regarding new features and bug fixes
  • Training emplyees and writing up tutorials on new features

After five years with the company I decided to leave and pursue a path in becoming a full stack developer. Currently, I am enrolled in a coding boot camp at UCI.
